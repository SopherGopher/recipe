Step One: Making the Batter

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a mixing bowl
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave safe bowl, once melted, whisk wet ingredients
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk together
  4. Let sit for ten minutes
  5. While batter is resting, dice the strawberries
  6. Fold the diced strawberries into the batter
