Two: Cooking the Pancakes

  1. Heat a pan to medium heat
  2. Add a thin layer of oil or butter to the pan so the batter doesn't stick
  3. Add a quarter cup of batter to the pan, depending on the size of the pan, you may be able to cook multiple pancakes at once
  4. Let the batter cook until you see small bubbles that burst on the surface
  5. Flip your pancake
  6. Once flipped, watch for small bubbles on the side of your pancake
  7. When you see the bubbles on the side bursting, remove and plate your pancake
  8. Enjoy your pancake, or proceed to the final step for optional plating recommendations
