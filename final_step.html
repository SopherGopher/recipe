Final Step: Toppings and Plating

  1. The most important part of this step is to have fun!
  2. Add your choice of toppings in any arrangement you like
  3. One classic recommondation would be:
    1. Create six evenly spaced whipped cream dollops
    2. Add a strawberry with the top cut off to each dollop
    3. Put a healthy serving of strawberry compote on top of your pancake stack
    4. Add fresh blueberries overtop the pancake stack and add a final dollop of whipped cream at the toppings
Bon Apetit!
